Spinach and Strawberry Salad

Prep. time: 10 min
Total time: 10 min
Yield: 8 servings
Whether you need a healthy lunch or are looking for a salad to bring to a party that – this is critical – people actually want to eat, this spinach and strawberry salad is the recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch spinach
  • 2 cups sliced strawberries
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

Directions

Step 1

In a large bowl, toss together the spinach and the strawberries.

Step 2

In a medium bowl, whisk together the oil, vinegar, sugar, paprika,
sesame seeds, and poppy seeds.

Step 3

Pour the mixture over the spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
